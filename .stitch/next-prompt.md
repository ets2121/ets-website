A high-fidelity, premium portfolio home page for "EasyTech Solutions" (ETS). The design should be "Premium Cyber-Obsidian," evoking technical mastery and innovative hardware/software integration.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Atmosphere: "Premium Cyber-Obsidian"—sleek, deep backgrounds with high-energy neon cyan accents. Professional, cutting-edge, utilizing glassmorphism and subtle atmospheric glows.
- Theme: Dark Mode (Void Deep Blue #0B1C38)
- Background: Void Deep Blue (#0B1C38)
- Surface: Obsidian Glass (#132240) with 80% opacity and 12px backdrop blur.
- Accent: Cyber Cyan (#00F2FF) for primary buttons, highlights, and icons.
- Text: Pure White (#FFFFFF) for headlines, Pike Silver (#B0C4DE) for body.
- Shapes: 12px-16px corner roundness for panels, 8px for buttons.
- Elevation: Depth through backdrop-blurs and soft radial glows.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Sticky navigation with "ETS Portfolio" logo, links (Services, Portfolio, Testimonials, About), and a "Contact Us" CTA button in Cyber Cyan.
2. **Hero Section:** High-impact section.
    - Headline: "Stop Wrestling with Manual Processes. Start Scaling with EasyTech."
    - Subheadline: "We turn manual delays into digital speed. From custom web apps to hardware automation, we build the simple, efficient tools your business needs to grow."
    - Primary CTA: "Get Your Free Consultation" (Cyber Cyan solid).
    - Secondary CTA: "See Our Solutions" (Outlined Cyber Cyan).
    - Visual: A high-tech mockup or abstract hardware/software visualization with a subtle "Cyber Cyan" glow behind it.
3. **Services Grid:** "The Engine powering your business transformation."
    - 3-column grid of glassmorphism cards.
    - Items: "Bespoke Web & MVP Development", "Custom Hardware Automation", "IT Support & Consulting".
    - Each card features a "Cyber Cyan" icon and descriptive text.
4. **Portfolio Showcase:** "Proof of our technological impact."
    - A masonry or bento-grid layout for project cards.
    - Projects: "Automated Inventory System", "IoT Smart Farm Control", "Clinic Queue Management".
    - Highlight the Solution and Result for each.
5. **Testimonials:** "Obsidian Glass" carousel or grid featuring client success stories.
6. **About Section:** "Premium technical agency with a focus on local growth. Showcases the core tech stack (React, Node, Arduino, etc.) with a "Cyber Cyan" accent.
7. **Footer:** Clean footer with Void Blue background and Cyan highlights.
