# Design System: ETS Portfolio (Obsidian Cyber)
**Project ID:** 8411991819688313538

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design system is "Premium Cyber-Obsidian." It combines the sleek, dark aesthetics of the obsidian void with high-energy neon cyan accents. The atmosphere is professional yet cutting-edge, utilizing glassmorphism, subtle glows, and precision-engineered layouts to convey technical excellence and creative innovation.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Void Deep Blue (#0B1C38):** Primary background for sections and page foundation.
* **Cyber Cyan (#00F2FF):** Primary accent color. Used for primary buttons, active states, icons, and decorative glows.
* **Obsidian Glass (#132240):** Surface color for cards and containers. Usually 80% opacity with backdrop blur.
* **Pike Silver (#B0C4DE):** Secondary text and labels. High legibility on dark backgrounds.
* **Pure White (#FFFFFF):** Primary headings and critical text for maximum contrast.
* **Success Emerald (#10B981):** Affirmative actions or status indicators.
* **Warning Amber (#FBBF24):** High-priority alerts or highlights.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Headings:** Bold, geometric sans-serif (Inter or Space Grotesk). Letter-spacing -0.02em for a compact, modern feel.
* **Body:** Clean, highly readable sans-serif (Inter). 1.6x line height for comfort.
* **Data/Labels:** Monospace or high-contrast sans-serif. Uppercase with 0.1em tracking.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:**
    * **Primary:** Solid Cyber Cyan (#00F2FF) with Void Blue text. 8px corner radius. High-intensity outer glow on hover.
    * **Secondary:** 1px Cyber Cyan border, transparent background. Pulse animation on hover.
* **Cards/Containers:**
    * Rounded corners (12px to 16px).
    * Subtly translucent background (#132240 at 80%).
    * 1px border at 10% white opacity for "edge lighting."
    * Deep, diffused shadows for elevation.
* **Inputs/Forms:** 
    * Recessed dark background.
    * Focus state highlights the border with Cyber Cyan and a subtle glow.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Structure:** 12-column grid with generous section spacing (120px+).
* **Whitespace:** Strategic use of "negative space" to focus attention on typography and central CTA.
* **Depth:** Multi-layered effects using backdrop-blur (12px to 20px) and soft radial gradients behind key components to simulate light refraction.
